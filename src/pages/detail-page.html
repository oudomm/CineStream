It is the Taisho Period in Japan. Tanjiro, a kindhearted boy who sells
charcoal for a living, finds his family slaughtered by a
demon. To make matters worse, his younger sister Nezuko,
the sole survivor, has been transformed into a demon herself.

Release Date: October 16, 2020

Status: Completed

Episode :26 episode (20–30 minutes)

Subtitles: English, Bahasa Indonesia, Bahasa Melayu, Deutsch, Chinese...

Genres : Action, Adventure, Drama, Fantasy, Horror, Mystery, Shounen

Main Character (Protagonist)

Tanjiro Kamado

Voice-over by : Natsuki Hanae (Japanese)
Zach Aguilar (English)

~ Kind and determined demon slayer seeking a
cure for his sister.
Uses Water and Sun Breathing.

Nezuko Kamado

Voice-over by : Akari Kito (Japanese)
Abby Trott (English)

~ Tanjiro's demon sister who fights to
protect humans without eating them.

Inosuke Hashibira

Voice-over by : Yoshitsugu Matsuoka (Japanese)
Hiro Shimono (English)

~ Wild, boar-masked fighter raised in the mountains.
Uses his own Beast Breathing style.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Voice-over by : Aleks Le (Japanese)
Bryce Papenbrook (English)

~ Scaredy-cat slayer who becomes super fast
and deadly when unconscious.
Uses Thunder Breathing.
